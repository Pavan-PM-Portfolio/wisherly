FAQ

Questions?
We've got answers.

Do I need to create an account?
+

No account needed to get started. Just open the builder, fill in your details, and share the link. Simple as that.

Can the recipient open it on their phone?
+

Absolutely. Every Wisherly surprise page is fully responsive and optimised for mobile. The confetti, animations, and unlock screen all work beautifully on any device.

How do friends add their wishes?
+

You share a separate contributor link with friends. They open it, type their name and message, and it gets added automatically — no account required.

Can I preview the page before sharing?
+

Yes! Once you've filled in the details and hit Create, your surprise page is saved and you're redirected straight to the live preview. You can share the link with anyone.

How long does the page stay active?
+

Free pages are stored locally. Premium and Pro pages are hosted on our servers and remain accessible forever with a shareable link.

Can I add my own music?
+

Yes — paste any MP3 or audio file — it will autoplay softly and loop in the background as they scroll through the surprise page.
